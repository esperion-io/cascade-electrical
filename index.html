MEET THE TEAM

Tradies You Can Rely On

Of Te Rarawa, Ngpuhi decent, Karl is born and bred in Aotearoa. Karl spent several years during his high school holidays working for THORN Lighting NZ, manufacturing light fittings and decided to become an electrician.

Karl spent over 15 years with Coll Electrical where he delivered several high value residential new builds. He was the project manager of multiple commercial jobs including Pak n Save Clendon, multiple New World stores across Auckland, and an Aotea Square infrastructure upgrade. He also spent his last 3 years as the Manager of the Coll Electrical Service Department, where he supported Watercare, Dress-Smart, and multiple property managers with their electrical requirements.

Karl also spent 3 years working for Sunshine Village Ski Resort, Canada, as part of the electrical maintenance team. He is a Qualified Inspector under EWRB and also holds an Auckland Council IQP licence for emergency lighting.
